# Sponsors & supporters

DISPATCH.AI is open source and built largely by one developer. Orchestrating and
testing AI agent workflows means running a *lot* of real model calls — and that
gets expensive fast. Sponsorship and contributions directly fund development,
testing, and infrastructure.

If DISPATCH is useful to you or your team, **any** help moves it forward —
sponsoring, contributing code, filing issues, or simply starring and sharing the
project.

## Backed by

DISPATCH.AI is backed under the **Pyca Ireland** non-profit initiative for tech
innovation to improve education. As part of that initiative, resources are
directed toward the research and development of the open-source DISPATCH.AI
project.

- **Pyca Ireland** — non-profit initiative for tech innovation in education.
- **Helios Labs**

- ⭐ **Star** the repo — it genuinely helps visibility.

- 🐛 **Report issues** — real-world bug reports are worth a lot.
- 💚 **Sponsor** — funding offsets API/testing costs. (Sponsorship links will be
  added here as they go live.)
